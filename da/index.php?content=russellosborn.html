Lucy Russell

Yale, Osborn b148

Contributor: Professor Michelle O'Callaghan

Date: early 17th century

Material: MSS

Description: Poem titles in the manuscript: 'An Eligie ffunerall one ye Death of Mrs Boulstred', ('Death I recant & say vnsaid by mee'), no separate title, continuation ('Death bee nott proude thy hand giues nott this blowe')

Source: Beinecke Library, Yale University Library, b148, James Osborn Collection (Osborn MS.), pp. 117-119

Permissions: From the James Marshall and Marie-Louise Osborn Collection, Beinecke Rare Book and Manuscript Library, Yale University
