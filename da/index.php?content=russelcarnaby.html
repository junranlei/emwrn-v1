Lucy Russell

Harvard, MS. Eng. 966.1

Contributor: Professor Michelle O'Callaghan

Date: c. 1620s-33

Material: MSS

Description: Poem titles in the manuscript: 'Another Elegie on the deathe of M:rs Boulstred', ('Deathe I recant, and say vnsaide by me'), no separate title, continuation ('Deathe be not proud thy hand giues not this blowe')

Source: Harvard University Library: MS. Eng 966.1 fols. 43-44v

Permissions: From the collections of Houghton Library, Harvard University
